AUTO-CERTIFY

About

With Auto-Certify certificate maker you can quickly generate certificates for work, school, sports and more.Choose your certificate template and generate certificates quickly and easily for required database.Auto-Certify saves your time from writing data to each certificate!!

Instructions

  • Select certificate template from your PC,add title and click on ADD NEW CERTIFICATE.
  • Now click on that template to start editing.
  • First select CSV file from your PC and click on UPLOAD CSV.
  • Click on draw blank and draw on the positions where data has to be printed on the canvas.
  • Now for each blank number select required heading and click on SUBMIT
  • Finally click on DOWNLOAD to download certificates for your complete database.
